About NETRA

NETRA (Network Eye for Threat Recognition & Analysis) was born from a simple but urgent question: Why do millions of people still fall for phishing attacks despite knowing they exist?

Our team of cybersecurity researchers and AI engineers built NETRA to bridge the gap between technical threat intelligence and everyday user protection. We believe security should be accessible, explainable, and proactive.

NETRA uses a multi-layered approach combining heuristic analysis, machine learning models trained on millions of phishing samples, and real-time threat intelligence feeds to achieve industry-leading accuracy.
